Our role is to exchange contact information between helper and job owner and for this reason we have an inspection fee of 45.- / This fee will not be added to the cost if you get the job, but will be deducted if you dont get the job.

Make sure you understand the job and expect the pricing to be reasnable and thatjob owner want you to go on inspection before you agree to inspection.
